摘　要　由于钢材的自身缺陷,以及加工工艺或设计等原因,会导致钢材存在裂纹,从而可能造成钢构件的脆性破坏。因此,
为了避免钢构件脆性破坏导致的严重后果,对钢结构中的潜在脆性破坏构件的重要性程度进行评估就显得尤为重要。通过
引入风险达成价值分析,对静定结构中的潜在脆性破坏构件的重要性程度展开研究,并提出了重要性程度的评估方法。并进
一步分析了构件总数量、相关系数、潜在脆性破坏构件的数量、可靠度指标等参数与潜在脆性破坏构件重要性程度之间的关
系和规律。结果显示:构件总数量、相关系数、潜在脆性破坏构件数量越大,潜在脆性破坏构件的重要性程度越大;构件的可
靠度指标越高,潜在脆性破坏构件的重要性程度越小。通过在工程实例桁架结构中的应用,不仅证明了所提方法的可行性和
有效性,也为潜在脆性破坏构件重要性程度的评估提供了理论参考依据。
关键词　潜在脆性破坏构件;重要性程度;静定结构;相关系数;失效概率

[Abstract]　Due to inherentdefectsin the steel, aswellasprocessing techniquesordesign issues, there may be cracksin the steel,
which can potentially lead to brittle failure ofsteelmembers. Therefore, to avoid the severe consequences caused by brittle failure of
steelmembers, itisparticularly importantto evaluate the importance degree ofpotentialbrittle failure membersin steelstructures. The
risk achievementworth wasintroduced to study the importance degree ofpotentialbrittle failure membersin statically determinate struc-
turesand an evaluation method wasproposed forthe importance degree. By analyzing the relationship between parameterssuch as the
totalnumberofmembers, correlation coefficients, the numberofpotentialbrittle failure members, reliability indices, and the impor-
tance degree ofpotentialbrittle failure members, relevantpatternshave been summarized. The resultsindicate thatasthe totalnumber
ofmembers, correlation coefficients, and the numberofpotentialbrittle failure members increase, the importance degree ofpotential
brittle failure membersalso increases. On the contrary, asthe reliability index increases, the importance degree ofpotentialbrittle fail-
ure membersdecreases. By applying the method to an engineering case trussstructure, itsfeasibility and effectivenesshave been dem-
onstrated, and a theoreticalreference forthe evaluation ofsuch issueshave also been provided.

failure probability
